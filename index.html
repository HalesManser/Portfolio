My love for horses started when I was very young. My family introduced me to Riding lessons when I was around 5 years old. I was very fortunate to study the art of Dressage under a professional trainer who was successful in the show ring and proven breeding line of Dutch Warmbloods. I grew up knowing nothing else than the love and passion towards this sport that my trainer had passed to me. I entered the show ring from the age of 6 to the age of 13, winning many of my classes! I then branched away from my training in dressage and began an internship with Harris Ranch where I was an understudy of the onsite equine herd health veterinarian. We worked with horses from the weaning age to 2 years old. We managed over 250 head with daily feeding, supplementing, medicating, and herd well being. I joined a very successful halter horse judging team in FFA and learned a vast knowledge about desired conformation. I began to expanded my knowledge and took on my own adventure in owning my own horses. I have done much research into equine health and nutrition and now have a passion to help educate and guide other equestrians to provide the best care to their horses!
My interest in Veterinary science began as soon as I can remember. I began my study in high school taking Agriculture biology, Animal sciences, and ROP Vet Science. I continued my learning at Fresno State with 2 semesters of Animal Sciences with an emphasis in Dairy and Hogs. I soon accepted a position with a Vippetcare and spend 7 years learning the most current practices of Preventative care.
I began my retail experience with Tractor Supply Co where I was on boarded as a FAST Team Member where my responsibility was to follow the guided plans for merchandising products for my home store. I soon was promoted out of this position to a Team Lead position where I was responsible for the customer experience and assisting the management team on the operation of the store. I was again promoted to Assistant Manager leading the sales floor and the sales team.
My coding experience is a new journey that I am excited to take on. I started coding with Bitwise and have successfully completed Websites for Beginners and now coming to the end of my second class Mobile Friendly Websites. I have enjoyed my journey so far and hope to continue in my study!
